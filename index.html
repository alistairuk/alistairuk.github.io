Alistair MacDonald

Hello, I am Alistair MacDonald, a maker with over two decades of hardware and software development experience. In recent years I have been specialising in the more unusual projects working with startups and artists, as well as teaching teaching my skills to undergraduates. I now mange FabLab Sunderland.

I am also a founding member of the Maker Space in Newcastle upon Tyne, an active member of the North East Maker community, and have a background in broadcast radio.
