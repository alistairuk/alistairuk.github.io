Alistair MacDonald

Hello, I am Alistair MacDonald, a maker with twenty years' experience of hardware and software development. I specialise in the more unusual projects often working with startups and artists.

For the last three years I have been supporting Interactive Media Design to undergraduates at Northumbria University and in 2016 I have been working as an Interaction Design Researcher.

I am also a founding member of the Maker Space in Newcastle upon Tyne, an active member of the North East Maker community, and have a background in broadcast radio.
